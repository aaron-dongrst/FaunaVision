# Training Videos (80% of your data)

Upload 80% of your pig behavior videos here.

## Folders:
- `tail_biting/` - Pigs biting other pigs' tails (distress indicator)
- `ear_biting/` - Pigs biting other pigs' ears (distress indicator)
- `aggression/` - Aggressive interactions (distress indicator)
- `eating/` - Pigs eating (normal behavior)
- `sleeping/` - Pigs sleeping (normal behavior)
- `rooting/` - Pigs rooting in substrate (normal behavior)

## Tips:
- Aim for 50-100+ videos per behavior class
- Use various lighting conditions and angles
- Videos should be 10-60 seconds long
