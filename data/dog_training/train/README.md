# Training Videos (80% of your data)

Upload 80% of your dog behavior videos here.

## Folders:
- `pacing/` - Dogs pacing back and forth (distress indicator)
- `scratching/` - Dogs scratching excessively (distress indicator)
- `sleeping/` - Dogs sleeping (normal behavior)
- `walking/` - Dogs walking normally (normal behavior)
- `resting/` - Dogs resting calmly (normal behavior)

## Tips:
- Aim for 50-100+ videos per behavior class
- Use various breeds, lighting, and angles
- Videos should be 10-60 seconds long
